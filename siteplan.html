Smart Study & Career Navigator

WDD 231 Individual Project Site Plan

1. Site Name

Smart Study & Career Navigator

This name was selected because the website helps students navigate both their academic journey and future careers. It combines study planning, career exploration, and skill development into one modern platform. The word "Smart" emphasizes technology-driven guidance and interactive tools.

Optional Domain: smartstudynavigator.com

2. Site Purpose

The Smart Study & Career Navigator website provides students and young professionals with career guidance, study planning tools, and skill development resources. Users can explore career pathways, discover required skills, create study roadmaps, and access educational resources through an engaging and interactive experience.

3. Scenarios

  • What skills and certifications do I need to become a Software Developer?
  • Which career path best matches my interests and strengths?
  • How can I create an effective study plan for my career goals?
  • What learning resources can help me improve my technical and professional skills?

4. Color Scheme

#1E3A8A (Deep Blue) – Used for headers, navigation, footer, and major sections.

#38BDF8 (Sky Blue) – Used for cards, highlights, links, and interactive elements.

#F59E0B (Amber) – Used for buttons, call-to-action sections, and alerts.

5. Typography

Poppins – Used for headings, titles, navigation menus, and featured sections.

Roboto – Used for paragraphs, descriptions, forms, and general content.
